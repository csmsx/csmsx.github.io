Phyto

Plant Phenotype API

Alpha version available with two phenotype functionalities.

Phyto automates plant phenotype analysis. Its simple interface allows any HTTP-compatible solution to leverage phenotype information.

Phyto is available as an online service. It is also embeddable for custom bundling.
