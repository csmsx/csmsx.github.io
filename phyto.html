Phyto

Plant Phenotype API

Phyto automates plant phenotype analysis. Its simple interface allows any machine or software connected to the Internet to leverage phenotype information.

  • Available online.
  • Embeddable for custom bundling and offline operations
  • Growing and learning phenotype analysis API:
    1. Leaf-area estimation
    2. Dominant and average color recognition (RHS and sRGB standards)
    3. 3D modelling for remote measurements (alpha version)

Please get in touch to know more.
